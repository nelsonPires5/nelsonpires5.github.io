About

I'm Electrical Engineering graduated at University of São Paulo. A Bahian who was born in São Paulo. I lived my entire childhood on the coast of Bahia, in Ilhéus and I have always been very curious and I first became interested in computing, starting to have contact in the area at the age of 9. Since then I have been learning various subjects in this world, from 3D animation to the internet of things. When it comes to work i try to put my skills into practice and also learn new skills, always seeking to help the team. Focusing on the growth of the organization and personal and professional development.
